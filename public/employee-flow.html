Employee User Flow

1. New Employee Registration

The first step for any new team member is to register their profile. This is done through the Staff Training portal.

  • Access the Registration Page: Navigate to staff-training/register-profile.html.
  • Company Password: You will first be prompted to enter a company-wide password (FNB-STAFF!) to ensure only authorized personnel can register.
  • Profile Creation: After verification, you will fill out your personal details, including name, email, a personal password for your account, phone number, primary store location, and start date.
  • Account Activation: Upon submission, your account is created in the system, and you are redirected to the training login page.

2. Staff Training Module

After registering, all employees must complete the mandatory training module.

  • Login: Use your newly created email and password to log in at staff-training/login.html.
  • Training Content: The training module consists of several sections covering company policies, food safety, and customer service standards.
  • Quiz Assessment: At the end of the training, you must pass a quiz to demonstrate your understanding of the material. Your quiz history is recorded.

3. Main Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is the central hub for daily operations.

  • Login: Access the dashboard via the main index.html page. Log in using the same credentials you created during registration.
  • Location Selection: After logging in, you must select your current store location to see the relevant data.
  • Dashboard Features: From the dashboard, you can access various modules:
    • Stock Management: Track inventory levels and manage stock.
    • Wastage Log: Record any food wastage.
    • Loyalty Management: Assist customers with the loyalty program.
    • Orders: View and manage incoming orders.
    • Suppliers: Access information about suppliers.

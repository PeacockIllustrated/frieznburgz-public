Employee User Flow

1. New Employee Registration

Welcome to the team! The first step on your journey with Friez n Burgz is to create your personal staff profile. This is all handled through our dedicated Staff Training portal, ensuring you have everything you need to get started.

  • Accessing the Registration Page: To begin, you can access the registration page by clicking here.
  • Company-Wide Password: To make sure our system stays secure and is just for our awesome staff, you'll first be asked to enter a company-wide password. This is a one-time step to get you into the registration area. The password is FNB-STAFF!.
  • Creating Your Profile: Once you're through the gate, you can set up your account. You'll fill in your full name, a login email, a unique password that you create, your phone number, your primary store, and your start date. This helps us get you set up in the system correctly.
  • Activating Your Account: After you submit your details, your account is instantly created! You'll then be guided directly to the staff training login page to start the next step.

2. Staff Training Module

We want to make sure you feel confident and prepared for your role. Our training module is designed to get you up to speed on everything Friez n Burgz.

  • Logging In: Head over to staff-training/login.html and use the email and password you just created to log in.
  • Exploring the Training Content: The training is broken down into a few friendly sections. You'll learn about our company's story and policies, important food safety practices, and our standards for providing amazing customer service.
  • Checking Your Knowledge: At the end of the training, there's a short, friendly quiz to help you check your understanding of the material. Your results are saved to your profile, so you can see how well you did!

3. Main Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is your command center for daily operations at the store. It's designed to be your go-to resource for all key tasks.

  • Accessing the Dashboard: You can get to the dashboard from the main index.html page. Simply log in with the same staff credentials you created earlier.
  • Selecting Your Location: To make sure you're seeing the right information for your store, you'll be prompted to select your current location right after logging in. This tailors the entire dashboard experience to your specific store.
  • Exploring Dashboard Features: The dashboard gives you access to a powerful set of tools to help with your daily tasks:
    • Stock Management: A real-time view of all inventory. Here, you can track what's on hand, see what's running low, and manage incoming stock deliveries.
    • Wastage Log: A simple tool for recording any food items that go to waste. This helps us stay efficient and reduce our environmental impact.
    • Loyalty Management: Your hub for all things related to our customer loyalty program. You can look up customers, add stamps to their cards, and redeem rewards for them.
    • Orders: This section allows you to view and manage all incoming orders, ensuring a smooth process from placement to pickup.
    • Suppliers: A handy reference guide with contact information and details for all our approved suppliers.

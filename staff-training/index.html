Introduction

Welcome to Friez&Burgz!

As a front staff member, you are the face of our restaurant, ensuring that every customer receives outstanding service. Your role is essential in creating a smooth and enjoyable customer experience.

This handbook is designed to provide you with clear instructions and structured guidelines for all aspects of your daily work.

Handbook Overview

This handbook is designed to help you work effectively. By following these instructions, you will contribute to maintaining the high standards of Friez&Burgz.

Let's work together to deliver fast, friendly, and flawless service every day!

Preparation

Morning Duties (Opening)

1. Store Environment

  • Remove chairs from tables, open shutters, turn on TVs, and clean all glass surfaces.

2. Music & Till

  • Play House Chill Music until 5 PM, then switch to Dance House. Ensure cash float is £150.

Night Time Duties (Closing)

1. Cleaning

  • Wipe down all surfaces, sweep and mop the floor, and empty all bins.

2. Restocking

  • Refill all three sauce fridges (Front, Backup, Large Back) using the FIFO method.

Service

Customer Service

Ensure every customer feels welcomed, valued, and satisfied. Always smile, maintain eye contact, and use a warm, friendly tone.

GOAL: Every customer should leave feeling like they had a great experience and want to come back!

Upselling Techniques

Sales Script

Greeting: “Hello! Welcome to Friez&Burgz. How's your day going?”

Offering Extras: “Would you like to add some bacon or pastrami to your burger?”

Everyday Rules

Key Everyday Rules

Mobile Phone Policy:

No phones during work hours except on your designated 20-minute break. Usage is monitored.

Staff Meals Policy:

One free meal and a can of pop per shift. Premium items are not included.

Performance Evaluation

Evaluation System

  • 90-100 pts: Excellent (Eligible for bonuses)
  • 80-89 pts: Good (Minor issues)
  • 70-79 pts: Needs Improvement
  • Below 70 pts: Unsatisfactory (Immediate corrective actions)
